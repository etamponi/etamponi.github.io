This blog is a place to think out loud about technology and philosophy — and the tension between them.

A few threads I expect to pull on:

- **Machine learning engineering** — practical lessons from building and deploying ML systems at scale. The gap between research and production is underrated.
- **Software craft** — design decisions, trade-offs, the kind of thing that doesn't fit in a README.
- **Philosophy of mind and AI** — what it might mean for a system to understand something, and why that question is harder than it looks.
- **The rite of passage** — as a friend put it, learning to write custom kernels is where things get interesting.

No fixed schedule. Posts appear when they're ready.

> The rite of passage these days is learning custom kernels.
> — overheard in a conversation about JAX
